# 📝 SECTION 11: DAILY PRACTICE EXERCISES

---

## Week 1: Foundation Building

### Day 1: Voice & Tone Practice
**Exercise:** Record yourself reading all scripts in Section 2  

- Listen back  
- Note: Are you speaking clearly? Too fast? Confident?  
- Re-record until you sound natural  

**Target:** 5 recordings  
**Time:** 30 minutes  

---

### Day 2: Email Writing
**Exercise:** Write 5 customer support emails  

- Pick 5 scenarios from Section 5  
- Write responses without looking at scripts  
- Compare to provided scripts  
- Identify gaps  

**Target:** 5 emails  
**Time:** 45 minutes  

---

### Day 3: Chat Speed Practice
**Exercise:** Type out chat responses  

- Use typing speed test website  
- Practice common chat phrases  
- Goal: **40+ WPM**

**Target:** 30 minutes of typing practice  
**Time:** 30 minutes  

---

### Day 4: Phone Role Play
**Exercise:** Practice phone scripts out loud  

- Call a friend/family member  
- Practice opening, holding, transferring, closing  
- Ask for feedback on clarity  

**Target:** 3 mock calls  
**Time:** 30 minutes  

---

### Day 5: Scenario Handling
**Exercise:** Random scenario practice  

- Pick 5 random scenarios from Section 5  
- Write responses in 10 minutes each  
- No looking at scripts!  

**Target:** 5 scenarios  
**Time:** 50 minutes  

---

### Day 6: Difficult Customer Simulation
**Exercise:** Angry customer role play  

- Have friend play angry customer  
- Practice de-escalation techniques  
- Stay calm under pressure  

**Target:** 3 difficult scenarios  
**Time:** 30 minutes  

---

### Day 7: Review & Reflection
**Exercise:** Weekly review  

- Review all scripts  
- Identify 3 weakest areas  
- Plan improvement for Week 2  

**Target:** Self-assessment  
**Time:** 30 minutes  

---

# Week 2: Speed & Confidence

## Daily Exercise (Mon-Fri)

1. **Morning:** Read 10 scripts out loud (15 mins)  
2. **Afternoon:** Write 3 email responses (20 mins)  
3. **Evening:** Type 5 chat responses (15 mins)  
4. **Before bed:** Review one difficult scenario (10 mins)  

**Total:** 60 minutes daily  

---

## Weekend

### Saturday
- 2-hour mock interview practice  
- Friend asks you all HR screening questions  
- Practice salary negotiation  

### Sunday
- Watch customer service YouTube videos  
- Take notes on techniques  
- Practice new techniques learned  

---

# Week 3-4: Real-World Simulation

## Daily Routine

**Monday:** Handle 10 imaginary customer emails (1 hour)  
**Tuesday:** Chat support simulation (30 mins typing + responding)  
**Wednesday:** Phone support - record 5 mock calls (45 mins)  
**Thursday:** Difficult customer scenarios (1 hour)  
**Friday:** Mixed scenarios - email + chat + phone (1 hour)  

**Weekend:** Full-day mock support shift (4 hours)  

---

# Typing Speed Improvement Plan

**Current:** ~35-40 WPM  
**Target:** 45-50 WPM in 30 days  

## Daily Practice (15 mins)

1. TypingClub.com or Typing.com  
2. Practice support-specific phrases:  

   - "Thank you for contacting us"  
   - "I understand your frustration"  
   - "Let me check that for you"  

3. Time yourself weekly  

---

## Progress Goals

**Week 1:** 35 WPM → 38 WPM  
**Week 2:** 38 WPM → 41 WPM  
**Week 3:** 41 WPM → 44 WPM  
**Week 4:** 44 WPM → 47+ WPM
