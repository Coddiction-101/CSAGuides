**Be Interview-Ready in 30 Days**

# WEEK 1: FOUNDATION (Days 1–7)

## Day 1: Self-Assessment & Setup

### Morning (1 hour)
- Read this entire notebook cover to cover
- Highlight sections you're weak in

### Afternoon (1 hour)
- Set up email signature
- Create sample responses document

### Evening (1 hour)
- Record yourself reading Section 2 scripts
- Listen back and note areas for improvement
- Practice 3 times

### Homework
- Research 5 companies you want to apply to
- Save job descriptions

---

## Day 2: Communication Basics

### Morning (1.5 hours)
- Study email templates (Section 6)
- Write 5 practice emails (different scenarios)
- Self-review for tone, clarity, professionalism

### Afternoon (1 hour)
- Chat scripts practice (Section 7)
- Type out 10 common chat responses
- Time yourself — aim for under 2 mins each

### Evening (1 hour)
- Watch 3 customer service training videos on YouTube
- Take notes on techniques
- Practice one new technique

### Homework
- Type 500 words at TypingClub.com
- Record your WPM score

## Day 3: Technical Troubleshooting

### Morning (1.5 hours)
- Study troubleshooting scripts (Section 10)
- Write step-by-step guides for 5 common tech issues

### Afternoon (1 hour)

- Watch intro tutorials

### Evening (1 hour)
- Practice phone scripts out loud
- Record yourself

### Homework

## Day 4: Salary & Negotiation

### Morning (2 hours)
- Study Section 4 thoroughly
- Write out YOUR salary negotiation script

### Afternoon (1 hour)

- Role-play salary negotiation with friend

- Get feedback

### Homework
- Watch 3 salary negotiation videos
- Take notes on tactics

## Day 5: Difficult Customers

### Morning (1.5 hours)

### Afternoon (1 hour)

## Day 6: HR Screening Prep

# Daily Habits to Maintain Throughout

## 1. Morning Affirmation (2 mins)

> "I am capable, confident, and prepared. I will ace this interview."

---

## 2. Typing Practice (15 mins)

- Use TypingClub or Typing.com
- Track progress weekly

---

## 3. Empathy Practice (Throughout the day)

- Listen actively to people around you
- Practice rephrasing what they say
- Build the habit of empathy

---

## 4. Self-Recording (10 mins)

- Record yourself answering 1 random question daily
- Listen for:
  - Clarity
  - Pace
  - Confidence
  - Filler words
- Improve daily

---

## 5. Gratitude Journal (5 mins before bed)

- Note **3 things you did well today**
- Note **1 thing to improve tomorrow**
- Celebrate small wins
