## 📞 SECTION 3: HR SCREENING CALL – FLOW

### Typical HR Screening Structure

| Step | Stage | Description | Duration |
|-----|------|-------------|---------|
| 1 | Introduction | HR introduces themselves and gives a brief overview of the company | ~2 minutes |
| 2 | Self Introduction | "Tell me about yourself" – your prepared 60-second introduction | ~1 minute |
| 3 | Background Questions | Questions about your education, skills, and experience | 5–10 minutes |
| 4 | Role Discussion | HR explains the role and asks about your interest in it | ~5 minutes |
| 5 | Salary Discussion | Discussion about your salary expectations and availability | 3–5 minutes |
| 6 | Your Questions | Opportunity for you to ask questions about the company or role | ~5 minutes |
| 7 | Next Steps | HR explains the next stage of the hiring process | ~2 minutes |

---

### ⏱ Total Call Duration
**20–30 minutes**
