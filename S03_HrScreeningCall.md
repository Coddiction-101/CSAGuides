## 📞 SECTION 3: HR SCREENING CALL – EXACT SCRIPTS

### Typical HR Screening Flow

| Step | Stage | Duration |
|-----|------|---------|
| 1 | HR introduction and company overview | ~2 minutes |
| 2 | "Tell me about yourself" | ~60 seconds |
| 3 | Questions about your background | 5–10 minutes |
| 4 | Questions about the role | ~5 minutes |
| 5 | Salary expectations discussion | 3–5 minutes |
| 6 | Your questions to HR | ~5 minutes |
| 7 | Discussion about next steps | ~2 minutes |

**Total Call Duration:** 20–30 minutes

---

## HR Question 1: Why Did You Choose BCA?

### Suggested Response

> I chose **BCA** because I've always been interested in how technology shapes our daily lives. During my **12th standard**, I was deciding between a pure IT path and something that also included business applications. BCA offered the perfect combination of both.
>
> The three-year program exposed me to areas like **programming, databases, web development, and system analysis**, which helped me build a solid technical foundation.
>
> More importantly, it helped me develop **problem-solving and logical thinking skills**, which are essential for any technology-related role, including customer support and technical troubleshooting.
>
> Looking back, I feel it was the right decision because it has prepared me well to **enter the tech industry and adapt to different technical roles.**
