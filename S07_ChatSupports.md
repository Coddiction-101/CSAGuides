# 🧠 Chat Support Best Practices

Good chat support focuses on **speed, clarity, and friendliness**.

### Key Guidelines

✅ Respond within **30–60 seconds**  
✅ Use a **friendly and conversational tone**  
✅ Use emojis **sparingly** (😊 👍 ✅ only professional ones)  
✅ Keep messages **short (1–3 lines)**  
✅ Use the **customer's name periodically**  
✅ **Confirm understanding** before moving to the next step  
✅ End with a **clear next action**

---

# 👋 Chat Opening Scripts

## Script 1: General Greeting

You:  
Hi **[Customer Name]**! 👋  
Thanks for contacting **[Company Name] support**.

You:  
How can I help you today?

---

## Script 2: Proactive Greeting (Customer browsing a page)

You:  
Hi there! 😊  
I noticed you're looking at **[Product/Page]**.

You:  
Can I help answer any questions?

---

## Script 3: Returning Customer

You:  
Welcome back, **[Customer Name]**! Good to see you again! 😊

You:  
How can I assist you today?

---

# 🔄 Chat Transition Phrases

## When Checking Something

You:  
Let me check that for you right away.

You:  
One moment please, I'm looking into this... ⏱️

You:  
I'm checking our system now. This will just take **30 seconds**.

---

## When Asking Customer to Wait

You:  
Thank you for waiting! ✅

You:  
I appreciate your patience while I looked into this.

You:  
Thanks for holding on! Here's what I found...

---

## When Escalating an Issue

You:  
I want to make sure you get the best solution. Let me connect you with our **[technical team / specialist]** who can help with this specific issue.

You:  
Your chat will transfer in just a moment. All your information will carry over so you won't have to repeat anything!

---

# 🏁 Chat Closing Scripts

## Script 1: Issue Resolved

You:  
Excellent! I'm so glad we got that sorted for you! 🎉

You:  
Is there anything else I can help with today?

Customer:  
No, that's all.

You:  
Perfect! Have a great day, **[Customer Name]**!  
Feel free to reach out anytime you need help. 😊

---

## Script 2: Pending Resolution

You:  
I've submitted this to our **[team name]** and you'll hear back within **[timeframe]**.

You:  
I'll also send you a summary email with:

✅ Your ticket number: **#[12345]**  
✅ Next steps  
✅ Timeline

You:  
Is there anything else I can help with in the meantime?

---

## Script 3: Customer Satisfaction Check

You:  
Before you go, how was your experience with me today? 😊

You:  
You'll receive a quick **1-minute survey** after this chat. Your feedback helps us improve!

You:  
Thank you for chatting with us today!

---

# 🔁 Handling Multiple Chats Simultaneously

When managing several chats, use quick **holding messages** so customers know you're still working on their request.

## Quick Holding Messages

- "Thank you! Let me check that for you."
- "One moment please..."
- "I'm reviewing your account now."
- "Just pulling up that information..."
- "Bear with me for **30 seconds** while I verify this."

---

# ⏳ Setting Expectations

Clear expectations prevent customer frustration.

You:  
"I'm checking with our **[team/system]** — this might take **2–3 minutes**.  
I'll update you every **60 seconds**!"

After 60 seconds:

You:  
"Still working on this for you! Should have an answer shortly."
