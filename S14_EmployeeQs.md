# ❓ SECTION 14: QUESTIONS TO ASK EMPLOYERS

## Why Asking Questions Matters

✅ Shows genuine interest  
✅ Helps you evaluate if company is right for you  
✅ Demonstrates you've thought about the role  
✅ Makes you memorable  
✅ Gets you critical information for decision-making  

---

# 15 Smart Questions to Ask

## About the Role

### 1. What does a typical day look like for someone in this role?

- Helps understand daily responsibilities  
- Shows you're thinking practically  

---

### 2. What are the key performance indicators (KPIs) I'd be measured on?

- Shows you're results-oriented  
- Helps you know what success looks like  

---

### 3. What tools and software will I be using daily?

- Practical question  
- Shows you want to prepare  

---

### 4. What's the team structure? Who would I be working with?

- Understanding team dynamics  
- Shows you value collaboration  

---

### 5. What's the typical ticket volume per day?

- Helps set realistic expectations  
- Shows you understand the workload  

---

# About Growth & Learning

### 6. What training is provided during onboarding?

- Shows you value learning  
- Indicates you want to ramp up quickly  

---

### 7. Are there opportunities to learn about other areas of the business?

- Shows curiosity beyond your role  
- Indicates growth mindset  

---

### 8. How does the company support professional development?

Certificate programs? Courses? Conferences?

- Shows long-term thinking  

---

### 9. What does the career progression typically look like from this role?

- Shows ambition  
- Helps evaluate long-term fit  

---

# About Company Culture

### 10. How would you describe the team culture here?

- Important for job satisfaction  
- Shows you care about fit  

---

### 11. How do you measure customer satisfaction, and how is that shared with the team?

- Shows you care about impact  
- Indicates data-driven mindset  

---

### 12. What's your favorite thing about working here?

- Personal question builds rapport  
- Gets honest insights  

---

# About Logistics

### 13. What are the shift timings? Is there shift rotation?

- Practical and important  
- Shows you're thinking about reality  

---

### 14. What's the remote work policy? How often is in-office required?

- Critical for work-life balance  
- Helps plan your routine  

---

### 15. What are the next steps in the interview process?

- Shows eagerness to move forward  
- Helps you plan timeline  

---

# When to Ask Questions

## HR Screening Call
Ask **2–3 questions**

- Focus on role specifics and next steps  
- Keep it brief  

---

## Technical / Manager Interview
Ask **4–5 questions**

- Dig deeper into role, team, growth  
- More detailed questions are okay  

---

## Final Round
Ask **3–4 questions**

- Culture, decision timeline, final clarifications  
- Can ask about offer details if appropriate  

---

# Questions to AVOID

❌ **"What does your company do?"**  
(You should know this from research)

❌ **"How much vacation do I get?"**  
(Sounds like you want time off before starting)

❌ **"When can I get promoted?"**  
(Too presumptuous for first interview)

❌ **"Will I have to work weekends/nights?"**  
(Sounds like you're not willing to be flexible)

❌ **"How soon can I work from home full-time?"**  
(Wait for offer stage to negotiate this)

❌ **Any negative questions about previous employees**  
(Comes across poorly)
