# 💬 SECTION 5: Customer Communication Scripts – 20 Common Scenarios

## 🧠 Framework for Every Response

Every customer interaction should follow this structure:

1. **Greeting + Acknowledge**
2. **Empathize** (if there is an issue/complaint)
3. **Provide Solution**
4. **Offer Additional Help**
5. **Close Professionally**

---

# SCENARIO 1: Customer Can't Log In

## 📧 Email Script

**Subject:** Re: Login Issue – Account Access Help

Hi [Customer Name],

Thank you for reaching out to us!

I understand you're having trouble logging into your account, and I'm here to help you resolve this quickly.

Let's try these steps:

### 1. Password Reset
- Go to our login page: **[URL]**
- Click **"Forgot Password"**
- Enter your registered email: **[customer@email.com]**
- Check your inbox for the reset link (also check spam folder)
- Create a new password

### 2. If you don't receive the reset email
- Reply to this email and I will manually send a secure reset link
- Check your spam/junk folder as well

### 3. Still having issues?
Please share:
- Any error message you see
- Screenshot if possible (hide sensitive info)

I'm here to help until this is resolved.

Best regards,  
**[Your Name]**  
Customer Support Team  
[Company Name]

# 🔑 Key Principles for All Responses

## Tone
- Professional
- Friendly
- Empathetic

## Structure
1. Greeting
2. Acknowledge
3. Solution
4. Offer help
5. Close politely

## Response Time

| Channel | Ideal Time |
|-------|-------|
Email | 2–6 hours |
Chat | <2 minutes |
Phone | <3 rings |

# 💬 Empathy Phrases

Use phrases like:

- "I understand how frustrating this must be"
- "I'm here to help"
- "Let's resolve this together"
- "Thank you for your patience"
- "I appreciate you bringing this to our attention"

---

# 🚫 Avoid These

Instead of saying | Say This
---|---
It's not my department | Let me connect you with the right team
That's our policy | Here's what we can do
You should have | For future reference
I don't know | Let me find out for you
Calm down | (Never say this)
