# 📧 SECTION 6: Email Writing Templates

## 🧠 Email Structure Formula

A professional support email usually follows this structure:

1. **Subject Line** → Specific + Actionable  
2. **Greeting** → Personalized  
3. **Opening** → Acknowledge receipt/issue  
4. **Body** → Clear explanation or solution with steps  
5. **Closing** → Offer additional help  
6. **Sign-off** → Professional signature with contact info

---

# ✉️ Template 1: Professional Email Opening

Hi **[Customer Name]**,

Thank you for contacting **[Company Name] Support**!

[Acknowledge their issue or question]

- **If it's an issue:**  
I apologize for the inconvenience, and I'm here to help resolve this as quickly as possible.

- **If it's a question:**  
I'm happy to help you with that!

# ✉️ Template 3: Apology Email

**Subject:** Our Sincerest Apologies – [Issue]

Hi **[Customer Name]**,

I want to personally apologize for **[specific issue]**.  
This is not the experience we want our customers to have, and I take full responsibility for helping resolve this.

### What happened
[Brief and honest explanation without making excuses]

### How we're fixing it
[Immediate action + long-term solution]

### What I'm doing for you right now
[Specific compensation or resolution]

I truly appreciate your patience and understanding. We value you as a customer and we're committed to regaining your trust.

Please feel free to reach out if you have any concerns.

Sincerely,  
**[Your Name]**

---

# ✉️ Template 4: Follow-Up Email

**Subject:** Following Up: [Original Issue]

Hi **[Customer Name]**,

I wanted to follow up regarding **[issue we discussed]** to ensure everything is working well for you now.

### Recap of what we did

✅ [Action 1 taken]  
✅ [Action 2 taken]  
✅ [Action 3 taken]

### Quick confirmation

Is everything working smoothly now?

If you're still experiencing any issues or have additional questions, please reply to this email and I'll assist you right away.

Thank you for being a valued **[Company Name]** customer!

Best regards,  
**[Your Name]**

# ✉️ Template 5: Escalation Email (To Customer)

**Subject:** Your Case Has Been Escalated – We're On It!

Hi **[Customer Name]**,

Thank you for your patience regarding **[issue]**.

I want to keep you updated: your case has been escalated to our **[Technical Team / Senior Support / Management]** so we can provide the best possible solution.

### What happens next

- **Priority Case Number:** #[12345]  
- **Assigned Team:** [Team Name]  
- **Expected Resolution:** Within [timeframe]  
- **Next Update:** I'll email you by **[specific date/time]**

Your case is a priority for us, and we're working diligently to resolve it.

If you have any questions in the meantime, please feel free to reply to this email.

Thank you for your understanding.

Best regards,  
**[Your Name]**

📞 Direct Contact: **[email / phone]**
