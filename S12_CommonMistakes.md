⚠️ SECTION 12: COMMON MISTAKES TO AVOID

Mistake 1: Robotic Responses  
❌ Bad:  
"Thank you for contacting us. Your issue has been logged. Ticket number 12345. Goodbye."

✅ Good:  
"Thanks for reaching out! I totally understand how frustrating this must be. Let me personally make sure this gets resolved for you today."


Mistake 2: Over-Promising  
❌ Bad:  
"Don't worry, I'll have this fixed in 10 minutes!" [Can't guarantee it]

✅ Good:  
"I'm working on this right now. It typically takes 15-30 minutes, but I'll update you in 15 minutes regardless."


Mistake 3: Technical Jargon  
❌ Bad:  
"You need to flush your DNS cache and clear your browser's HTTP cookies stored in the local storage."

✅ Good:  
"Let's clear some temporary files that might be causing this. I'll walk you through it step-by-step."


Mistake 4: Not Listening  
❌ Bad:  
Customer: "I tried that already..."  
You: "Okay, please try it again."

✅ Good:  
Customer: "I tried that already..."  
You: "I understand you tried that. Let me try a different approach..."


Mistake 5: Defensiveness  
❌ Bad:  
Customer: "This is terrible service!"  
You: "Well, it's not our fault. It's the shipping company's problem."

✅ Good:  
Customer: "This is terrible service!"  
You: "I sincerely apologize. Regardless of where the issue originated, I'm taking responsibility for fixing it for you."


Mistake 6: Ending Too Quickly  
❌ Bad:  
"Okay, problem solved. Bye!"

✅ Good:  
"Great! Is there anything else I can help you with today? I'm here if you need anything!"


Mistake 7: Not Following Up  
❌ Bad:  
"I'll escalate this." [Never follows up]

✅ Good:  
"I've escalated this. I'll email you an update within 24 hours, even if it's just to say we're still working on it."


Mistake 8: Making Excuses  
❌ Bad:  
"We're short-staffed today, so responses are slow."

✅ Good:  
"I apologize for the wait. Let me help you right away."


Mistake 9: Ignoring Emotions  
❌ Bad:  
Customer: "I'm so frustrated!"  
You: "Okay, what's your ticket number?"

✅ Good:  
Customer: "I'm so frustrated!"  
You: "I completely understand your frustration, and I'm sorry you're going through this. Let's fix this together."


Mistake 10: Guessing Instead of Admitting You Don't Know  
❌ Bad:  
"I think it should work... maybe try this..."

✅ Good:  
"That's a great question. I want to give you accurate information, so let me check with our technical team and get back to you in 10 minutes."
